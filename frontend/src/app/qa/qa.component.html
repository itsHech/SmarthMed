Questions and Answers

How can I book an appointment?

Booking an appointment is simple! Just sign up on our platform, choose your preferred doctor, and select an available time slot that suits you.

How long does it take to get a confirmation?

Once you book an appointment, you will receive an instant confirmation. You will also get reminders via email or SMS before your appointment.

Why choose our platform?

Our platform offers an easy and secure way to connect with experienced healthcare professionals, manage medical records, and receive online consultations, all from the comfort of your home.

Are there any service fees?

Consultation fees vary based on service type. Some services may be covered by insurance. You can check the pricing details before booking your appointment.
